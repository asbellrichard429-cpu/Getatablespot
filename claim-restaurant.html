Under 5 minutes. Fill in the form, complete payment, and you're live. No hardware, no integrations, no tech team needed.

Log in to your dashboard from any phone or computer. Update your wait time in one tap. It shows up in real-time on GetATableSpot for nearby diners.

Yes. Cancel from your dashboard or by emailing us. No notice period, no cancellation fees. Your listing stays active until the end of your billing period.
