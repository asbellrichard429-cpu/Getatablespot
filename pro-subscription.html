How does the AI concierge work? +
The AI knows every restaurant currently loaded near you — including their real wait times, ratings, cuisine, price level, and whether they have outdoor seating. You tell it what you need in plain language and it picks the best match and explains why.
How do wait alerts work? +
You save restaurants to your favorites and set your wait threshold. When a restaurant's estimated wait drops below that number you get an instant alert. You can set different thresholds for different restaurants.

The annual plan is $2.24/month billed as $26.88/year — saving you $8.88 compared to monthly. If you're going to use it regularly (and you will), annual is the better deal.
What cities are supported? +
GetATableSpot works in any city worldwide with Google restaurant data — which is essentially everywhere. Simply enter your city, state, or zip code and we pull real restaurants near you.
